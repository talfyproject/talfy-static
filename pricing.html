Frequently Asked Questions

How does the billing work for annual plans? ▼
Annual plans are paid monthly at the discounted rate, with a 12-month commitment. You get 50% off the monthly price and can renew your credits anytime they run out.
What happens when I run out of profile credits? ▼
When your credits are exhausted, you can immediately renew your plan to get more credits. Your unused credits never expire and remain in your account forever.
Can I change my plan anytime? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle, and your existing credits will remain available.
Do my credits expire if I don't use them? ▼
No! Your profile credits never expire and remain in your account forever. This makes our plans perfect for companies with seasonal hiring needs.
Is there a free trial available? ▼
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new users with 5 free profile credits. No credit card required to start your trial.
