Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller
This website is operated by Talfy, which is the data controller for all personal data collected through the platform. For any request or clarification, you can contact us at privacy@talfy.com.

2. Types of Data Collected
We may collect the following categories of personal data: identification data (name, email), professional data (job title, experience, expected salary), login credentials (encrypted), company information (for recruiters), and any data voluntarily uploaded (CVs, logos).

3. Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing
The processing of data is based on consent, performance of a contract, and legitimate interest. Data is used to create and manage profiles, connect talent with companies, provide platform services, and ensure security and compliance.

4. Processing Methods
Data is processed using IT tools and stored on secure servers, with appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect integrity and confidentiality.

5. Data Retention
Personal data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined, or until users request deletion. Accounts inactive for 24 months may be deleted after prior notice.

6. User Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase your data, restrict processing, object, and request data portability. To exercise your rights, write to: privacy@talfy.com.

7. Cookies
We use cookies for essential functionality and analytics. For more info, refer to our separate Cookie Policy.

8. Data Security
We adopt advanced security measures such as encrypted communication, limited access, and anomaly detection systems to protect your personal data.

9. Third-Party Services and Transfers
Your data may be shared with third parties strictly necessary for platform operation (e.g. hosting, analytics) and may be transferred outside the EU with adequate safeguards.

10. Updates to this Policy
This privacy policy may be updated. Changes will be posted here with the last updated date. Continued use of the site implies acceptance of such changes.

11. Contact
For any questions or to exercise your privacy rights, contact: privacy@talfy.com
