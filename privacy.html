Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2025

1. Who We Are

Talfy is a digital platform operated by [Legal Entity Name], based in [Address], providing recruitment and talent-matching services. You can contact us at: privacy@talfy.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect different types of personal data depending on your role:

  • Candidates: name, email, job title, professional experience, expected salary, tools known, languages, uploaded CVs, profile photos, IP address, and browser/device info.
  • Companies: company name, VAT number, logo, sector, login email, search behavior, IP address.

3. How We Collect Your Data

Data is collected via registration forms, profile completion, cookies, analytics tools, contact forms, and when users interact with the platform.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent – for optional features (e.g. newsletters, marketing).
  • Contract – for service delivery to candidates and companies.
  • Legitimate Interest – for platform security, analytics, service improvement.
  • Legal Obligations – in case of audits, tax laws, or user reports.

5. Purpose of Processing

  • To allow user registration and authentication.
  • To manage and show anonymous candidate profiles to companies.
  • To improve our services and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

6. Use of Cookies and Tracking Tools

We use cookies and similar technologies for login persistence, analytics, and UX improvements. A dedicated Cookie Policy is available with details and opt-out options.

7. Data Retention

Personal data is stored for no more than 24 months of inactivity. Backups may be retained for an additional 6 months. Users can request deletion at any time.

8. Who We Share Your Data With

We may share data with:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers (e.g. Render, AWS)
  • Analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Plausible)
  • Payment processors (for company subscriptions)
  • Legal authorities (if legally required)

9. Data Transfers Outside the EU

If data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we ensure adequate safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses, DPA agreements).

10. Your Rights Under the GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Receive a copy of your data (portability)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority (Garante Privacy in Italy)

11. Profiling and Automated Decisions

We do not use your data for automated decision-making that significantly affects you. Candidate visibility is based on filters applied by companies.

12. Children

The platform is not intended for minors under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If discovered, such data will be deleted.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be published here, and when significant, we’ll notify you by email or platform message.
